Privacy Policy for Chauhan Dental Laboratory

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Chauhan Dental Laboratory, we prioritize the privacy and security of the personal and medical information shared with us by our dental partners and their patients. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information provided to us.

1. Information Collection

We collect information necessary for fabricating custom dental restorations and appliances. This may include patient names, case numbers, physical impressions, digital scans, and clinical photographs provided by dental practices. We also collect contact information from our dental practice partners (name, email, phone number, clinic address) for business communication.

2. Use of Information

The information provided is strictly used to fulfill prescription orders, fabricate dental products, clarify case requirements, and manage invoicing and accounting. We do not use patient information for marketing purposes.

3. Data Protection and HIPAA Compliance

We implement robust physical and digital security measures to protect the confidentiality of all patient records and digital impressions. We adhere to industry standards regarding the privacy of protected health information (PHI) and work securely with dental professionals.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or patient data to outside parties, except as required to fulfill our services (such as shipping carriers) or if compelled by law.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@chauhandentallaboratory.com.
