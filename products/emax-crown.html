About E-max Crown

IPS e.max® is widely regarded as the ultimate material for cosmetic, all-ceramic dental restorations. Made of premium lithium disilicate glass-ceramic, these crowns feature a unique combination of realistic translucency, high flexural strength (400-500 MPa), and outstanding biocompatibility. They are designed to blend seamlessly with surrounding natural teeth by copying their exact shade gradients and light refraction properties.

E-max crowns are processed using either digital CAD/CAM milling or hot-pressed glass technology. Since they are 100% metal-free, they avoid dark margin lines and give anterior restorations an incredibly natural, high-end cosmetic finish.
